Tacos Al Pastor

I have been eating Tacos al Pastor for all my life, the slow seared meat is spun around to get cooked from all sides, making ripe, juicy, and delitous meat. The beef is topped with onions, cilantro, and roasted pinapple, all put over a fresh corn tortilla. This, toped with salsa and other such toppings, is hands down one of the best meals in the world.
